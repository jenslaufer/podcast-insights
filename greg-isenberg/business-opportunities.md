# Business opportunities

> Every concrete business/startup idea across the notes, grouped with its
> monetization. Tag **🟢 = audience-light** (harvests existing demand — fits a
> no-audience builder; see [`../distribution-without-audience.md`](../distribution-without-audience.md))
> vs **🟡 = needs distribution/audience** to work. Source episode in *(parens)*.

## A. Vertical data products (Firecrawl micro-SaaS) — 🟢

Take a $100s/mo horizontal tool, rebuild **one slice for one niche**, sell the
output (charge per result, not per seat). Vibe-coded in a weekend, 95–99% margins.

- **Sneaker-resale price tracker** (StockX/GOAT/eBay alerts) — **$50/run or $500/mo** *(firecrawl)*
- **Niche SEO gap finder** ("you rank for 12, they rank for 47") — **$200–500/mo** *(firecrawl)*
- **Filtered grant/RFP/lead alerts** by fit score ("nobody wants 300M, they want the 50") — **$29/mo** *(firecrawl)*
- **Niche research reports** (crypto/biotech) sold to VCs/PE — **$1,000–5,000/mo** *(firecrawl)*
- **Real-estate comp-report agent** (listings + tax records + permits → comp in 30s) — **$300/mo** *(firecrawl)*
- **Amazon/product review-trend tracker** ("battery complaints up 40%") — **$99/mo** *(firecrawl)*
- **B2B lead enrichment** (find founders + emails → enriched CSV) — **$100–500/batch (~$2 tokens)** *(firecrawl)*
- More verticals to claim: real-estate pricing, SaaS competitor tracking, FBA tracker, finance ops *(firecrawl)*

## B. AI agents sold as "AI employees" — 🟢

Sell an outcome-producing worker, not seats. "Agents are the new SaaS."

- **Managed AI employee** (OpenClaw agent **~$5K/mo**, Hermes agent **~$10K/mo**), unlimited usage, sold as a hire *(1m-solo-agent)*
- **Vertical computer-use agent** for one industry (manufacturing, real estate, distributorships) — the big B2B opening *(making-money-openclaw, 9-opportunities)*
- **Niche autoresearch "always-on experiment engine" retainer** — **$5K/mo** + performance bonus *(karpathy)*
- **TikTok trend-hunting agent** built per client *(making-money-openclaw)*
- **Upwork AI-automation jobs** — **$500–5,000/job**, demand already posted *(making-money-openclaw)*
- **Agent-in-Slack for a niche** (Genspark Claw, ~$25/mo setup) *(start-ai-agent)*
- **Junior-employee replacement** ("Juniors" / junioremployees.ai — junior, not senior) *(9-opportunities)*
- **48-hour custom software** for non-tech SMBs — **$5K instead of $80K** (med-spas, contractors, clinics); the interview prompt *is* the sales call *(you-are-using-fable-5)*

## C. Agent-run flipping & brokering — 🟢

Same motion: **flip, broker, or relaunch** against a cheap/abandoned asset.

- **Dead-domain flipper** (DR 20+, niche keywords, clean history) *(start-ai-agent)*
- **"Should-I-even-call" deal screener** on BizBuySell / Acquire.com listings *(start-ai-agent)*
- **Restaurant / local-business lead tool** *(start-ai-agent)*
- **Buy & refactor SaaS** (mine churn/support tickets, refactor, flip or run) *(you-are-using-fable-5)*
- **Contract-refund firm** — cross-ref vendor contracts vs invoices (auto-renewals, unused seats, price escalators); **take 25% of savings** *(you-are-using-fable-5)*
- Target: a **$200–1,500/day** cash-flowing business *(start-ai-agent)*

## D. Niche directories (programmatic SEO) — 🟢

**$2K–10K/mo passive** on **$200–1,000** build cost. Site is easy (Claude Code);
the hard part is sourcing the data. Keyword-first, product-second.

- Worked niches: **jetski rentals** (74K/mo, KD 13), **dog beaches** (60.5K/mo, KD 4),
  **pediatric dentists** (201K/mo, KD 1), **luxury restroom-trailers** (~$273/day, built in 4 days) *(online-directory)*
- Monetize: display ads → **sell qualified leads** to listed businesses → **upsell a vertical SaaS** tool *(online-directory)*

## E. Consumer / mobile apps (gotcha feature) — 🟡

Needs a viral feature + paid/creator distribution.

- **AI mobile app with a "gotcha feature"** — WrestleAI hit **100K+ downloads / ~$200K**; niche apps do **$50–1,000/mo** *(making-money-ios)*
- **AI interior-design app** (faceless, agent-run marketing) — **$700–1,000 MRR** on ~60 sec/day *(openclaw-go-viral)*
- **Health/nutrition vertical** — "23andMe meets Cal AI"; Cal AI does **$50M ARR**; extend to sending food / certified chefs *(9-opportunities)*
- **Pet monitoring** — industry **$140–150B, <2% have smart monitoring**; build the "Huberman-sponsor" pet version + $30 AI add-on *(9-opportunities)*
- **Agent-first rebuilds of known tools** — Greg's favorite category ("100+ ideas"); mobile-first + agent + niche + willingness-to-spend + boring workflow *(9-opportunities)*

## F. Community, IRL & events — 🟡

Recurring revenue, but audience/curation is the moat.

- **Niche Discords** — "Dads play [game/hobby]" → the dad company *(9-opportunities)*
- **Premium retreats / events** — an art retreat made **$90–110K vs the typical ~$5K** purely on audience fit; **virtual event $60K for 3h/day over 2 days**; lamp-making meetups → **$7–8K premium** tiers *(9-opportunities)*
- **Third spaces / loneliness** — physical venues with paid memberships *(9-opportunities)*

## G. Media as distribution — 🟡

- **Live, unscripted business shows** ("Twitch for business") — **$70–100K/mo**; TBPN **sold >$100M** on low viewership *(9-opportunities)*

## H. Ambient / holding-co / "one-hour company" — 🟢/🟡

- **Ambient businesses** — agents monitor, find, execute, support; you check in every few days. Greg expects **7–8 figures** *(23-ai-trends)*
- **One-hour company / micro-monopoly** — 5,000-person niche → app in 48h → **100 customers × $50/mo, agent-run → ~$60K profit, 1 person** *(23-ai-trends)*
- **Holding company of agent-run niche businesses** in adjacent niches, each with a ghost team *(23-ai-trends)*
- **"Glassdoor of AI agents"** — directory/reputation layer for the agent economy *(23-ai-trends)*
- **Convert legacy SaaS to outcome pricing** — or build outcome-native startups (Gartner: 40% of enterprise SaaS shifts to outcome-based by 2030) *(23-ai-trends)*

## I. Local-AI / privacy & continuity — 🟢

Only possible now that frontier intelligence runs on-device/air-gapped.

- **On-prem / air-gapped agent for sensitive data** — sell to lawyers, doctors, therapists, anyone handling confidential info *(claude-fable-5-banned)*
- **Provider-continuity / fallback** — "what happens to our AI workflows if our provider gets cut off?" Sell the local fallback *(claude-fable-5-banned)*
- **Synthetic focus-group firm** — judge 50 ad variants overnight with personas from real reviews — **~$3,000/launch, ~$100 tokens** *(you-are-using-fable-5)*

## J. SaaS-to-exit (the wrapper play) — 🟡

- **Vibe-code a niche SaaS → ~$50K/mo at ~50% margin → sell at 10–15× EBITDA = a $3–5M business.** Loop: build audience → pre-sell → automate → repeat → compound *(saas-minting)*

## Shortlist for a no-audience builder

If you want **traction + fast feedback without an audience**, the 🟢 categories that
harvest existing demand are the fit. Fastest to a paying yes/no:

1. **AI-employee / automation service on Upwork** (B) — demand is already posted; close with a demo.
2. **Vertical data product** (A) — one niche, sell the output per-result; validate with 10 cold emails.
3. **48-hour custom software / contract-refund / focus-group firm** (B/C/I) — service revenue, the prompt is the sales call.
4. **Niche directory** (D) — slow feedback but near-zero CAC and passive; the compounding play.
